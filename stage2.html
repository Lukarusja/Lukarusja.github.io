Activity

If you haven't got your ideas yet theres a few activities to try.
The first is to brainstorm university relevent areas you want to work on, study skills, time management, that sort of thing to get some ideas for an academic goal, then do the same for a life skill goal.
Maybe you could make a mind map with a split between academic and life skills.
Once you have your goals, consider asking people you know for feedback, maybe they can point out something you hadn't thought of and improve your goals!
Try not to change them entirely though!
