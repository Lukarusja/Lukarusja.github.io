Introduction


Hi, we are so glad to have you here! In this series of five activities we will go through each stage of the RGS Lanyard. What is it, you ask? Let us talk you through each stage one by one!



Stage 1


Stage 1 is all about enhancing your self-awareness. During it you will have to reflect on your past and all the feedback you have had and build a consistent story you will follow during the later stages. Fun fact, you are in this stage right now!




Stage 2


During Stage 2 you will set yourself a goal you want to be working towards. That is the main focus of this entire course, so think really hard on what you want to achieve!




Stage 3


This is why the goal you set in Stage 2 was so important - it is the main focus of this and the next stages. During this one you will have to reflect on your goal




Stage 4


As said in the previous stage, you will think even more about your goal during this one!




Stage 5


Here you are going to actually start working on your very well thought out goal through the means you come up with during this stage





Let's get started


Since now you know how each stage is going to look like, we can finally get started!
